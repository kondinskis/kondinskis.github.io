Remote control for Popcorn Time

Bored of standing up to change subtitle, volume, movie or TV show? Well this is a remote control which will make your life easier allowing you to change from distance.

How to set it ?

Open popcorn time, go to settings click on 'Show advanced settings' find 'Remote Control' and enter the values from popcorn in the app settings or click on 'Generate Pairing QR code' and scan it with the app QR reader.
